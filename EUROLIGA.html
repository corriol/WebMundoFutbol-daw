In the summer of 2000, Europe's top club competition turned the page and entered a new era. Since then, 17 years have passed, during which basketball fans from the old continent and beyond have enjoyed a rich spectacle of teams and excellent players that have starred on the courts of the best basketball league outside the NBA. From the first match in the Euroleague that took place in Madrid between Real and Olympiacos on October 16, 2000, to the last final in Istanbul (May 21, 2017) between Fenerbahce and Olympiacos, 3,814 have been played. Matches! During this period, the organizing authority has introduced seven different formats, always seeking the best possible result and the pleasure of millions of competition fans. The number of teams that have participated in the modern era of the Euroleague amounts to 83 (19 different countries), while 2,700 players have graced the tournament with their presence. Only four franchises have competed in all 17 seasons. They are Barcelona, ​​Olympiacos, Baskonia and Zálgiris Kaunas, the most faithful participants in the best European league.
